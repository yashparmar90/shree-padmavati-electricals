About Us

Shree Padmavati Electricals is a trusted wholesaler of all types of electrical accessories and parts, committed to delivering quality products at competitive prices. With strong industry knowledge and reliable supply, we serve retailers, contractors, and industrial clients with dependable electrical solutions.

We deal in a wide range of electrical products including switches, wires, cables, holders, MCBs, distribution boards, lighting accessories, connectors, fittings, and other essential electrical components. Our focus is on supplying durable, safe, and high-performance materials that meet industry standards.

At Shree Padmavati Electricals, customer satisfaction is our priority. We ensure bulk availability, timely delivery, and consistent quality, making us a reliable partner for all electrical wholesale needs.
